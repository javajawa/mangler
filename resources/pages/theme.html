The ‘Cookie Policy’ icon is the ‘Cookies Icon’ by Fat Cow WebHosting, licensed under the CC-BY-3.0 license.

Fonts

The fonts Marcellus SC and Nunito are included from the Google WebFonts API. Amusingly, this is one of the few parts of the website which stop it getting a perfect Google Page Speed score, due to Fonts API not specifying cache validators.
